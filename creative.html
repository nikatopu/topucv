So, Let's Fly Far...

 Only the poem part of this animation was done by me. Animation was done by my sister - Mariam Topuridze, and the music by my brother - Ioane Topuridze.

 The idea of the poem came to me from watching a simple video describing the differences between two types of caterpillars, who basically live the same lives but can turn into different types of creatures after development.

 Working on this poem was quite interesting. The writing was, well, usual to say the least. What made it interesting was working with my siblins afterwards to create something of ours. Later on I was even tasked to translate it to ENGLISH, which, I think, I did quite well.

Bruises of a White Dove

 This is one of the poems which I greatly admire. I do admit that at some points I believe I could have done better, written better, but I still love it.

 The way I got the idea to write this poem was an outcome of two different situations. Firstly, I was writing a large poem, one containing lots of chapters, when I came across a scene of an opera song, so I needed to make something emotional, something that would get into people's hearts. Secondly, I was recently going through media, watching all of the wild animalistic things unhold in the world, when I came to a realization, that I was chained to it, chained to the ground and the people that were free - were hated.

 This poem is a great one, because it is a product of two personal problems crashing into eachother. I am glad that I was able to work in a problematic situation and come up with a creative poem that resolved the given problems.
